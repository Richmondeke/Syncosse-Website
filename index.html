Our Story

Our journey began in 2021, working at the heart of the transport and logistics industry. We saw firsthand the systemic challenges shaping the sector: high driver turnover, talent shortages, and concerns around driver well-being. These pain points weren’t just operational setbacks but deep-rooted issues impacting both drivers and businesses. This experience drove us to seek sustainable solutions.

2023: A Deeper Dive

Determined to understand these challenges, we conducted extensive market and user research, uncovering the root causes of dissatisfaction. Poor working conditions, lack of engagement, and minimal support for physical and mental health were driving the turnover crisis.

Why We Exist

At Syncosse, we believe the success of any organization begins with the health and happiness of its employees. Our solutions are designed to reduce turnover, promote well-being, and enhance productivity, ensuring that drivers—who are the backbone of the logistics industry—feel valued and supported.
